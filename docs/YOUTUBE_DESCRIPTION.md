## 🛡 WHY PRIVACY MATTERS HERE

Most AI writing tools (Grammarly, QuillBot, ChatGPT, etc.) send your text to their servers, where it may be stored, analyzed, or used to train future models. With Wrytica:

→ Your text only ever reaches **your own API key provider** (e.g. Google for Gemini)
→ Wrytica itself **never sees, stores, or touches** your content
→ Use Ollama locally and **zero bytes** leave your machine
→ No account required. No terms that let us use your data. Open source, auditable by anyone.
